ABOUT ME

Born and raised in Kerala, I discovered the world of design while trying to find something to do besides engineering and medicine. I am also interested in game development, both art and code.

I keep telling my friends that I have no worries in my life that can't be solved by a few laps at a pool. That's probably why my motto in life is "Keep calm and eat, sleep and swim." Apart from swimming, I also play chess, read and, of course, watch movies.

My favourite TV series is Mr. Robot, which I tend to watch continuously on repeat. The perfect way to end my day is to have a milkshake.
